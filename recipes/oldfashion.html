Old Fashion Recipe

The Classic Drink

At around 200 years old, this drink is definitely considered a class. Usually created with Bourbon, this drink is both sweet and seasoned.

Even though this drink is quite old, it still is one of the most popular drinks of the current day.

Ingredients

  • Bourbon: 2 ounces
  • Sugar: 1 tsp
  • Water: 1 tsp
  • Bitters: 3 dashes of your favorite
  • Orange (or lemon) twist for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the sugar and bitters to a glass (highball recommended). Add water and stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Fill the glass with large ice cubes (or those giant square cubes), add bourbon, and gently stir to combine.
  3. Slice a section of the orange pill and squeeze over the liquid. Rub the rim of the glass with the orange pill and add to drink.
  4. Enjoy a classic!
